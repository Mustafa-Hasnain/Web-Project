ABOUT US

Our Story

20 years ago, Founder and CEO, Glacious nibba opened the first Glacious Garments store in a little shop in Zamzama. The idea for a store like Glacious garments was conceived in the mind of this pioneering, pure patriot, nostalgic about the old craft of the hand-woven material. Glacious, the brand, was always rooted but possessed an ambitious spirit. Ever changing, evolving and expanding. By 2002 it was making women’s wear and soon expanded into mens wear, kids wear and added many affiliate brands with Glacious Garmnets website launching in 2008. The year 2012 saw Glacious change into a mega retail brand, when it decided to evolve with the customer and introduce colourful lawn prints. Innovative and experimental, Glacious introduced furniture, bedsheets, bags, accessories, shoes and soon became a complete lifestyle brand. In just two decades, Glacious nibbas 400sqft store transformed into a 44-store enterprise stretching across 17 cities in Pakistan. Making a mark –on-the-ground and online – Glacious Garments has now expanded to four countries across the globe. Despite the brand’s rapid and colourful metamorphosis, one thing remained constant: Glacious love for craft and its effort to keep it alive. The design philosophy was always colour, craft, culture and heritage. glacious drew inspiration from across the globe to become a global ethnic brand.
